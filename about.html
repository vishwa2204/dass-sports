ABOUT Dass Physical Training and Sports Academy

Welcome to Dass Physical Training and Sports Academy, a Top Sports Academy in Cuddalore. With over seven years of unwavering dedication to excellence, we are proud to be the go-to destination for athletes and fitness enthusiasts alike. Our story is one of passion, commitment, and a relentless pursuit of sporting greatness.

Our Founder, C. Dass
At the helm of Dass Physical Training and Sports Academy is our esteemed founder, C. Dass. With a vision deeply rooted in the world of sports and fitness, Mr. Dass brings his wealth of experience to the forefront. As a visionary and an ex-Army veteran, he understands the value of discipline, dedication, and the pursuit of excellence.

Our Journey
Our journey began over seven years ago with a simple yet profound goal: to provide a platform for individuals to discover their athletic potential and lead healthier lives. Over the years, our dedication to this vision has remained unwavering.
